Is account registration required?

Account registration at BiH Banking is required in order to apply for the loan.

Is there any registration fee?
No, account registration is completely free for all users.

Where can I learn more about BiH Banking?
You can check out on our BiH Malaysia’s social media channels. We will be sharing a lot of latest updates and announcements there.

What if I am not able to find my answer here?
You can contact us at bcs@bih.com.my or reach us through our social media channels.

Who is eligible for this loan application?
This loan application is mainly for all Malaysia startup companies.

What is the main purpose of this loan application?
To help startups or companies in growing their business with our loans.

What should I do if I want to apply for a loan?
There are 3 main steps that you need to complete. First, fill up the basic information of your company including company name, type of business, etc. Second, upload your company’s balance sheets and income statements for the past 3 months. Lastly, fill up the business model strategy of your company.

Will I be notified if my application is approved or declined?
You will be notified via email on the status of your application, whether it is approved or declined. If your loan is approved by the bank, you will receive an Approval Letter stating the payment due date, installment amount, tenure, loan amount approved and the loan account number.
